The web is not as open as it used to be: big monopoly platforms have formed new proprietary layers on top of it. But with your help, we will fix this. We are creating a better architecture for the web. We break the “you get our app, we get your data” package deal with a cross-origin data storage protocol, separating data servers from webservers.

Unhosted is a non-profit project, made possible by the awesome people on this page (and the ones who didn't want to be mentioned). To know more, subscribe to, or browse through our mailing list. Our project is now one year old. We'll put some more info on here on as soon as we finish writing it.
