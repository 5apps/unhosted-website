Unhosted

Apptorrent

Why?

  1. UX: Apps are the new bookmarks (the new hyperlinks).

  2. Freedom from Apple, Google, GitHub

  3. Robustness (many copies of popular apps)

  4. Resilience against DNS killswitch (freedom of speech)

  5. Peer distribution (viral and democratic)

  6. Peer reward (e. g. Flattr buttons)

  7. Bringing the right to fork and patch to the open web
