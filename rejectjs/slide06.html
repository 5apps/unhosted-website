Introducing Unhosted Accounts

Unhosted Identity: OpenId(+Webfinger)

  • OpenId already does this for identities

  • Please accept OpenId on your website. Your users should be allowed to choose their own online 'surname'. Webfinger makes it actually usable

  • Some sites still offer Single-Origin Webfinger, but Friendika, Diaspora, StatusNet and WordPress already fixed this.

  • HTTP response header: Access-Control-Origin-Allow: *
