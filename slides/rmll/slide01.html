Introducing Unhosted Accounts

From Installed Software to Hosted Software

  • Software Example: Use your computer to invite people to your birthday.

  • 15 years ago: Operating system, drawing program, text editor, spreadsheet, dead tree slices, snail mail.

  • 10 years ago: Operating system, drawing program, text editor, spreadsheet, email program.
