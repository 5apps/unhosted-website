Imagine personal data freedom...

Unhosted is an open web standard for decentralizing user data. On the unhosted web, data is stored per-user, wherever the user decides.That's where it belongs.

Download Unhosted SDK alpha

The web is evolving

In the last few years, the web has slowly changed from just being a source of information to something entirely different.

Today, the web is a place where you can share your thoughts, your experiences and your life with other people; a place to keep things that are important to you.

What happens to your data?

Whenever you post content to the web (images, text, video, music...), it is kept on the servers of that particular application.

This means that your online data is stored in centralized servers belonging to various companies, meaning it's not really yours any more.

Bringing data back to the people

What if there were a way, that not only ensures security, freedom and control over your data, but also provides a better experience for both users and developers while resolving some of the other issues of the current web architecture?

This is where Unhosted kicks in...
